What this is

A private online studio for women who want calm, expert guidance on what truly suits them.

Iki Atelier is a virtual beauty guidance studio offering 1:1 color analysis and makeup coaching. It is built for women who feel overwhelmed by products, trends, and generic tutorials, and who want guidance that is specific to their face, their coloring, and their life.

The session is not a tutorial. It is a structured conversation that ends with a clear, personalized picture of what works for you and why.

Women who want to understand their colors, their features, and their technique with more clarity and confidence.

04

Women who want to feel more like themselves — not more made up.

What happens in the session
I
Audit A short review of your goals, current routine, and what has and has not worked. This is where the session is calibrated to you.
II
Color and feature analysis A personalized read of your undertone, season, contrast level, and the features that shape what works on your face.
III
Practical coaching Technique guidance, product direction, and adjustments built entirely around your face and your needs — not a generic approach.
IV
The Iki Guide A personalized digital guide with your analysis, notes, and recommendations. Yours to keep.
The makeup can be removed. What suits you stays.

You leave the session with more clarity, more confidence, and a better understanding of what truly suits you — one you carry with you long after the makeup comes off.
